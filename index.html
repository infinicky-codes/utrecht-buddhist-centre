About Us

We are a new Utrecht based community dedicated to the practice and teachings of Buddhism, offering a space for meditation and spiritual growth.
Our centre is open to everyone, regardless of background or experience. We offer regular meditation sessions, teachings, and community events.
We're just one of many centres of the Triratna Buddhist Community, an international Buddhist movement based in over 20 countries.
We teach and practise an approach to Buddhism that is suitable for everyone living in today's world.
